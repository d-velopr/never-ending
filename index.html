// What I handle

The whole machine behind the music — so the artist can stay an artist.

01

A&R & Artist Development

Finding the sound, shaping the rollout, picking the singles, and building a release cadence that compounds instead of fizzling. The long game, run on purpose.

02

Publishing

Getting compositions administered and collecting the money the work already earns — mechanical and performance royalties most independent artists never claim.

03

Splits & Royalties

Clean splits agreed and documented before the song blows up. Producers, features, writers — everyone paid right, no friendships ruined over a hit.

04

Song Registration

ISRCs, PRO and publishing registration, metadata done correctly. The boring paperwork that decides whether 5 million streams actually pay out.

05

Marketing & Instagram

Turning catalog into reach — content strategy, Instagram growth, and the promo cycles that put records in front of the people who'll run them up.

// Case study

YFEGUERO — built independent, from Northeast LA.

YFEGUERO has been making music seriously since he was sixteen. The first record he ever cut with his engineer DG — "Henny" — became the breakout. No label put it there. No advance funded it.

Since then it's been a body of work, not a fluke: the 8-track project TrashBags & Foreigns (Oct 2025), a deep catalog of singles, and a release schedule that's still active in 2026. A wavy, West-Coast-meets-Florida street sound with a real local scene behind it.

My job on this one: turn that catalog into infrastructure — registered, administered, marketed, and paid. The numbers below aren't projections. They're the dashboard.
